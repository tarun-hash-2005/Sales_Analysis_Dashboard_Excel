# Sales_Analysis_Dashboard_Excel

"Interactive Sales & Profit Dashboard using Excel to analyze regional performance, product trends, and business insights."

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Slicers for interactivity
- Dashboard Design & Formatting

---

## 📈 Key Features of Dashboard
- 💰 Total Sales and Total Profit overview
- 📅 Year-wise sales comparison (2023 vs 2024)
- 🌍 Region-wise sales analysis
- 🛒 Category & Product-wise performance (Treemap)
- 📊 Monthly order trend analysis
- 💳 Payment mode distribution (Cash, Card, UPI)
- 📉 Quarter-wise sales trend
- 🎛 Interactive filters (Region & Category slicers)

---

## 📊 Business Insights
- Identified top-performing categories and products
- Analyzed regional sales contribution
- Observed monthly and quarterly sales trends
- Evaluated customer payment preferences

---

## 📂 Dataset Details
The dataset includes:
- Order Date
- Region (East, West, North, South)
- Category & Product
- Sales Amount & Profit
- Payment Mode
